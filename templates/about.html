About Aquarium

OUR MISSION STATEMENT

Aquarium Genius is an online information website all about the aquarium hobby. Currently the page is centered around freshwater aquariums, fish and live plants to provide you with the best information to make your hobby more enjoyable. Aquarium Genius is best known for the detailed answers that provide just the information you are looking for.


Our story

In December of 2014 we bought the domain name AquariumGenius. Since then, thousands of words have been written to answer questions and the website has been growing steadily. More and more people read our articles every single day. The site provides information to people on a daily basis. Bart Sprenkels started the business after learning a ton about aquariums and wanting to share the obtained knowledge with others. Using the internet it was possible to reach a large audience in an incredibly user friendly way. Using mostly Google, readers are able to find just exactly what they are looking for without much effort. While the site experienced a slow start, around the summer of 2019 some articles started to gain traction in Google and were climbing in the search results. This was perfect! More people were able to find the website, and a lot of people spend some time on the website to find what they are looking for. In the last months of 2019, we decided to expand. Hopefully, in the start of 2020 more experienced writers will join Aquarium Genius to provide more information for all aquarium owners.
