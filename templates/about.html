OUR MISSION STATEMENT

Shutter Fever Photography is a professional, full service photography studio based in Charlestown, Co Mayo. We thrive on creating beautiful visual imagery – whether for a wedding couple, commercial brand or photograph for the walls of a family home, we are constantly driven to create the next amazing image to delight our clients. A family run Mayo business, we have a keen appreciation for what photography is all about. Our work is constantly evolving, but that timeless significance of quality photography is not lost – capturing memories, one click at a time.
